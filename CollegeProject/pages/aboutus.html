About Us This is a project based tourism website designed by the group of final year student from Parul University.

Vision

For Nepal to be a world renowned destination and It is a website that is dedicated to travel. This Site is based on reviews,best tourist destination at a given place,booking of vehicles and hotels and hiring of tourist’s guide.

Mission

To market and develop tourism to and within Nepal that exceeds our visitors experience expectations, delivers value to our stakeholders, improves the living standard and sustain the cultural values and way of life of our people and enables broad base participation of Nepalese in the tourism industry
